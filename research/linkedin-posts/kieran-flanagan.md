## Post 3 — 6 days ago (June 2025)

Ok, Fable 5 is out. Your feed is full of buzzwords — "insane", "game changing", "everything is dead". But if you're a marketer, what can you actually do with it?

Fable 5 is for big, meaty, multi-hour problems where you give it a goal and leave it to do lots of work. Here are real use cases where you'll get value:

1. Mining your customer data for the one insight that matters — feed it thousands of survey responses, CRM notes, call transcripts, and chat logs. Fable has an edge because of how much context it can hold and synthesize.

2. Multi-source GTM research in a single pass — pull analytics, walk through your pricing page, review competitor reviews on G2, and get a recommendation on positioning and pricing.

3. Audit your entire website for messaging consistency — read every page, flag where positioning contradicts itself, where value props change wording, and where CTAs don't match page intent. One report with page-by-page recommendations.

Keep your current model for: writing copy, quick back-and-forth work, anything that isn't big multi-step or large context.
