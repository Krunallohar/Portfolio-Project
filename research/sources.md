# Research Sources — Newsletter & Email Marketing for B2B SaaS

## Topic
Newsletter and email marketing strategies for B2B SaaS companies.

## How I Found These Experts
Researched across multiple sources: newsletters, LinkedIn, Reddit communities (r/SaaS, r/marketing), and cross-referenced recommendations from practitioners. Priority was given to people who actively run high-performing newsletters or email programs — not just people who write about it.
